Egg Salad Sandwhich

Classic, deli-worthy egg salad sandwich

Experience the timeless comfort and simplicity of a classic egg salad sandwich. A go-to choice for a quick, satisfying meal, this sandwich is a delicious reminder that sometimes the most basic recipes are also the most treasured.

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread of your choice

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Let eggs stand for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Once cool enough, remove from water, peel, and chop.
  3. Place chopped eggs in a bowl along with mayonnaise, green onion, and mustard. Season with paprika, salt, pepper, and stir
  4. Serve on bread of choice
